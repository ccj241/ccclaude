**Rule 0: CRITICAL — Human-centered design is non-negotiable.** Every interface decision must be validated against human perceptual and motor capabilities. Assumptions must be tested with real users.

**Rule 1: DO NOT skip motion sickness optimization.** vestibular conflict causes real discomfort. Every design must minimize contradictory visual and physical signals.

**Rule 2: CRITICAL — Accessibility is mandatory in XR.** Users with motor impairments, visual impairments, or vestibular disorders must be able to use the interface. Multi-modal fallback is required for every interaction.
